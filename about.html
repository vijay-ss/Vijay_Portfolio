About Me

This is my story.

My name is Vijay Saddi and I am currently a Senior Data Analyst in the Telecommunications industry. I have a BSc in Psychology from York University and a Post-Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics.

I first became interested in data after my internship at Reliance Foundation in Bombay, India. I worked in the Health Information Team, where I was involved in many projects involving heavy use of customer data. At this point I didn't have a background on performing basic analyses, but found it extremely interesting to manipulate. At this moment I knew I wanted to build a career in Data.

With the wealth of resources online, I gradually started learning the field, starting with some basic Python code and building from there on. Since then, I have completed several certificates including: IBM's Data Science Specialization and Python for Data Science and Machine Learning. Since then, I have applied my knowledge by completing some relevant projects such as a Covid-19 Outbreak Tracker dashboard, and market research for a business proposal.

This is my jorney to date, with the main goal of helping to drive the industry forward by changing lives.
